title: "Add the Loops Form to Typedream"
---

To add the Loops form to your typedream page, use an “Embed” comp

![](/images/typedream-1.png)

Copy your form snippet from your Loops forms page and paste it in as a code embed:

![](/images/typedream-2.png)

You should be all set! Please note, our form endpoint has rate limiting and you may see issues if you try to submit the form multiple times in quick succession.
